# Every throw, measured.

TrueLine turns your iPhone into a bowling ball-motion tracker. Prop your phone behind the approach, bowl, and get the numbers a coach would give you — ball speed, board at the arrows, launch angle, breakpoint, entry angle, and hook — for every throw.

Everything runs on your phone. No account, no cloud, no subscription — one purchase, yours forever.
